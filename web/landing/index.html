What the model is being built to do

Classification that remembers its language

The first public layer focuses on keeping definitions, domains, evidence, and uncertainty attached when Synthia moves between scientific lexicons.

Biology classification

Organize biological observations, taxonomic terms, and source-linked definitions without losing the domain context that produced them.

PhyloCode memory

Preserve how names, clades, redescription traces, and expert review should remain visible beside formal classification work.

Neutrosophic uncertainty

Carry bounded T/I/F values, indeterminacy sources, and plithogenic contradiction signals as traceable classification metadata.

Lexicon switching

Let one task move between biology, taxonomy, physics, math, and AI governance without flattening the meaning of the terms being used.

Public boundary

Research model, not a production authority

Synthia is currently an educational research project. It is not a production scientific authority, medical tool, environmental deployment system, drone-control platform, or formal nomenclatural authority. Its role is to preserve context, evidence, uncertainty, and lexicon boundaries for human review.
